Walchand Linux Users' Group

Club Information

WLUG is an active technical club which was established in 2003. It basically promotes the open-source technologies and always strive to explore the new technologies.
WLUG was formed by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering and Information Technology, WCE Sangli. The club mainly consists of students from UG with Computer Science / IT as major. Every year, the club arrange 3 to 4 national events based on Linux, Shell, Python, Android Development, Docker, Cryptocurrency etc.
